Leave Policy - TechVision Company

Annual Leave:
- Each employee is entitled to 30 days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave must be requested at least 3 days in advance.
- Unused leave can be carried over to the next year up to 10 days.

Sick Leave:
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- A medical certificate is required for sick leave longer than 2 days.

Hourly Leave:
- Employees can request up to 4 hours of hourly leave per month.
- Hourly leave must be approved by the direct manager.

Public Holidays:
- All national public holidays are considered paid days off.
